Johnson & Johnson
Asia Pacific Marketing Manager, Beauty Care

Part of the Global Franchise Organization (GFO) responsible for managing 2 global beauty care equities, Aveeno and Clean&Clear, across the Asia Pacific (AP) region, supporting a Marketing Director with key focus on:

  • Brand strategy development and deployment for 12 Asia-Pacific markets
  • Responsibility for Brand Equity and implementing the Brand footprint and toolkit
  • Development of the Innovation pipeline with 5 year horizon, based on Quantitative/Qualitative consumer validated research in key markets.
  • Driving alignment of brand plans within a matrix organization, with both US and priority AP markets India, Indonesia, China and Korea.
  • Leading execution of Innovation projects across functions, geographies, and levels including R&D, design, market research, packaging & engineering, finance, regulatory, and operations teams

Marketing Manager
